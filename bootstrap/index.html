United States

Home to the Motor City of Detroit itself, the United States has birthed countless beautiful sports cars from big burly Chargers, to smaller but zippy hatchbacks such as the Focus ST.

First-Gen Mustang

First produced in 1964, the Ford Mustang was the first of the American "pony car" era, featuring a long hood and short deck which was very popular at the time.

  • Horsepower: 105
  • Torque: 156 lb/ft
  • Displacement: 2.8 L
C8 Corvette

Eighth generation of Chevrolet's flagship sports car, the C8 Corvette showcases innovation in its engine placement. The newest Corvette is now a mid-engine sports car, sharing the likes of European exotics such as Ferrari and Lamborghini.

  • Horsepower: 670
  • Torque: 460 lb/ft
  • Displacement: 5.5 L
Challenger SRT

Definitely the heaviest out of the three, coming in over two tons, the Dodge Challenger SRT is a force to be reckoned with. With all that weight comes a lot of horsepower paired with a supercharger to ensure drag race wins.

  • Horsepower: 840
  • Torque: 770 lb/ft
  • Displacement: 6.2 L
